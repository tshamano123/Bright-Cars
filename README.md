#🚗 Bright Car Sales Data Analysis

##📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Bright Car Sales data to uncover business insights that support strategic decision-making. The analysis was conducted using sales, revenue, pricing, and regional performance data from manufacturing years 2014–2015.

The main objective of the project was to identify:

-=Top-performing car brands and models
-=Revenue trends over time
-=Best-performing regions
-=Pricing and margin patterns
-=Opportunities for business growth

The project combines data cleaning, SQL analysis, data visualization, and dashboard development to transform raw data into actionable business intelligence.

##⚙️ Methodology / How the Case Study Was Done
-=1️⃣ Data Collection & Import

The dataset was imported into Databricks for data cleaning and analysis.

-=2️⃣ Data Cleaning & Transformation

-=Data preparation included:

Handling missing values
Renaming columns
Formatting dates
Creating calculated fields
Aggregating sales metrics

##Key Insights Found

-=💰 Revenue Performance
Total revenue reached approximately $7.49B.
Ford generated the highest revenue at $1,338M.
Chevrolet and Nissan followed as top-performing brands.

-=🌍 Regional Insights
Florida (FL) was the strongest-performing state with $1,133M in revenue.
The top three states were:
FL
CA
PA

-=📈 Sales Trends
Sales performance showed clear seasonality patterns.
Q1 of the 2015 manufacturing year recorded the strongest performance.

-=🚘 Pricing Insights
The correlation between price and mileage was -0.56, indicating that higher-mileage vehicles tend to sell at lower prices.

-=📉 Profit Margins
Honda had the strongest margin performance.
Dodge showed the weakest margins, highlighting opportunities for pricing improvements.

-=📊 Year Comparison
Revenue increased significantly from 2014 to 2015.
2015 revenue exceeded $6.8B compared to $594M in 2014.

##📌 Conclusion

This project successfully transformed raw car sales data into meaningful business insights through data analysis and visualization. The interactive Power BI dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify trends, and make informed business decisions.

The analysis demonstrated how data-driven strategies can improve revenue growth, operational planning, and profitability.
